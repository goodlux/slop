## Technical Implementation Notes

The slop.at platform now extracts these **defined terms**:
- `Person` - individual human beings
- `Organization` - companies and institutions  
- `Place` - geographic locations
- `Event` - occurrences and happenings
- `Meeting` - scheduled gatherings
- `Activity` - actions performed
- `Conference` - professional symposiums
- `DefinedTerm` - technical vocabulary
- `Topic` - subject matter
- `Family` - familial groups
- `Community` - social networks
- `Company` - business entities

Each entity type gets its own color highlight, making the semantic structure visually apparent. The **GLiNER2** model handles extraction, **Oxigraph** stores the graph, and **FastAPI** serves it all up with beautiful **markdown** rendering.
